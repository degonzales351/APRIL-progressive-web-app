Description

Applied Pregnancy Informatics provides expecting mothers with daily check-ups and notifications to ensure wellbeing of both mother and child.

Daily Check-Ups provides helpful information related to current symptoms and offers resources to expecting mothers.

Informative community

All Users will find peace of mind knowing that we provide top quality resources ensuring baby's state of health comes first and foremost.

Pre conditions

Users must download the app off the web

Users must create an account and answer questions related to their health and state of pregnancy.

  • Informative community resources will connect Alyssa to top quality health care services.
